About Me

As a career Governess Nanny, your children are my main focus and drive.

I am a highly educated private in-home Governess Nanny, Parenting Coach, Trainer, and Organizer committed to delivering personalized, nurturing, and educational childcare, supporting the unique needs of individual children and families.

I am experienced with newborns, young children, twins, and various family dynamics, focusing on care, education, nutrition, health, and development.

With my flexible schedule, I provide guidance and assistance while increasing parents’ free time, optimizing family time, and creating an overall harmonious home life.

Valid US Passport | NY State Driver’s License | Professional Certifications

History / Background

I’ve been providing childcare, in various ways and to various degrees, for over 30 years, including non-nanny positions.

Starting out as a peer mentor in elementary school and continuing through college, I gained a great deal of insight into a diverse population of varying ages.

I still continue being involved in mentor programs with school-aged children and young adults. I’ve branched into developing community programs as well, all of which is incredibly rewarding.

At 10 years old I began as a babysitter, became a camp counselor in my teens, and eventually worked as a religious school teacher in my early 20’s.

Later, I moved into helping overwhelmed families in my spare time, assisting parents with daily childcare. My passion for children fueled my independent studies in everything child-related, and I eventually moved into nannying as my full-time career without looking back!

I love my career with children, and I look forward to the many more adventures I have yet to experience with it!
